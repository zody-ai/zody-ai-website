Positioning statement

Zody AI Ltd is a UK software company building practical digital tools for business and workflow automation. The company maintains a public website describing its services, policies and contact routes, and appoints a responsible individual to oversee compliance, information handling and external due diligence responses.

The responsible individual is expected to understand the standards that apply to the software, ensure the business answers compliance questions accurately, and coordinate corrective action if gaps are identified.

  • Company name used consistently across website, contracts, invoicing and support channels.
  • Public contact details available for banks, partners and regulators.
  • Evidence of registration supplied using Companies House number and other official references where needed.
