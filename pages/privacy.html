Privacy Policy

This policy explains how Zody AI Ltd collects, uses and protects personal data in connection with its website and custom software services, effective April 2026.

1. Who we are

Zody AI Ltd is the controller of personal data processed through its website and software services, except where another party is clearly identified. Company number: 17132898. Registered office: Fairview Farm Foots Lane, Burwash Weald, Etchingham, England, TN19 7LE. For general enquiries contact hello@zody-ai.uk; for support contact support@zody-ai.uk.

2. What personal data we collect

  • Account information such as name, email address and authentication identifiers.
  • Usage and device information required to operate, secure and improve the service.
  • Customer-submitted content and records processed through the product.
  • Support correspondence and incident reports.

3. Why we process personal data

  • To provide and maintain the software service.
  • To authenticate users and protect accounts.
  • To respond to support requests and service issues.
  • To comply with legal obligations and defend legal claims where necessary.

4. Lawful bases

Processing personal data to deliver our custom software services is primarily based on contract — we need to process account and usage data to provide and maintain the service. We rely on legitimate interests to monitor service performance and improve security, on legal obligation where law requires us to retain records, and on consent where we have specifically asked for it, such as for optional marketing communications.

5. Sharing

Personal data may be shared with hosting providers, infrastructure vendors and professional advisers where necessary to operate the service. Data is not sold. Direct marketing requires an appropriate lawful basis and, where required, consent.

6. Security

Reasonable technical and organisational measures are used to protect data, including encryption in transit, access control and activity logging where appropriate.

7. Retention

Data is retained only for as long as necessary for the purpose for which it was collected. As a guide: account and service data is held for the duration of the subscription and for six months after termination; financial and transactional records are retained for seven years in line with HMRC requirements; support correspondence is kept for three years; operational and security logs are retained for twelve months. Data subject to a deletion request is removed as soon as reasonably practicable, except where retention is required by law.

8. Your rights

Individuals may have rights to access, rectify, erase, restrict, object to processing and receive a copy of their personal data, subject to legal limits.

9. Contact

For privacy questions or to exercise your rights, contact privacy@zody-ai.uk. For general support, contact support@zody-ai.uk.

This policy does not constitute legal advice. Seek independent legal guidance where required.
