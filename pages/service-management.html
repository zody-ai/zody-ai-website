Service management practices

How Zody AI Ltd monitors the service, receives incident reports, manages breaches and keeps support routes visible.

MonitoringIncident responseBreach notification

Questions this page answers

Can customers report a security issue easily?

Yes — security concerns, vulnerabilities and operational issues can be reported directly to security@zody-ai.uk. The address is published on this page and in the footer.

What happens in a breach?

We maintain documented escalation routes, decision-making roles and external notification steps so the right actions happen quickly and consistently.

How is service health monitored?

Operational logs and alerts are in place to surface failures early, before they become prolonged customer-impacting incidents.

Operational stance

Zody AI Ltd operates software with supportability in mind. The business maintains a dedicated contact route for security concerns, keeps incident information centrally recorded, and prepares to notify the right parties if a breach or serious incident affects customer data.

  • Security concerns can be reported to security@zody-ai.uk.
  • Operational logs and alerts support investigation and root-cause analysis.
  • Personal data incidents are assessed promptly against legal and contractual notification obligations, with the director accountable for the response.
