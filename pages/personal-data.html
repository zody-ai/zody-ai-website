Handling personal data

How customer data is collected, protected, segregated and made portable, in line with UK GDPR expectations for software providers.

UK GDPREncryptionRBAC

Questions this page answers

What personal data do you process and why?

The business explains categories of personal data, the purpose of processing and its lawful basis.

How is data protected?

Personally identifiable data and access tokens are encrypted in transit and at rest, and customer data is segregated.

Can customers export or delete their data?

Customers can request export, correction or deletion of their personal data through our support channel.

Data protection commitments

Zody AI Ltd aims to process only the personal data needed to provide the service, support users and maintain secure operations. Customer data is handled under documented privacy controls, kept separate between tenants or accounts, and restricted through role-based access so staff only see the minimum necessary for their job.

  • Encryption for data in transit and sensitive stored data.
  • Documented privacy notice covering categories, purpose and lawful basis.
  • Account-level separation to reduce the risk of cross-customer access.
  • Processes for export, correction and deletion requests.
  • No storage of third-party platform credentials when OAuth is used — tokens only, with appropriate scoping and lifecycle management.

Internal access

Access to customer data is restricted through role-based access control, with approvals required for elevated access and all access reviewable on request.

Customer rights

Customers can request a copy of their data, correction of inaccuracies, or deletion. Retention periods and any legal holds are explained in the privacy policy.
