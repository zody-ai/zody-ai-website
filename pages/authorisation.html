Authorisation model

Zody AI Ltd designs customer authorisation around direct user consent and least-privilege access. Where third-party platform integrations are involved, the preferred model is OAuth — customers authenticate directly with the platform and grant scoped access tokens to the software. We do not ask customers to share their platform credentials with us, and we do not store credentials we have no business retaining.

  • Customer data is processed on servers located in the EU West region (Amsterdam, Netherlands).
  • Privacy and terms pages are accessible from every page of the site via the footer.
  • OAuth token lifecycles and customer permission scopes are handled securely and reviewed periodically.

Hosting and infrastructure

Customer data is processed on servers in the EU West region (Amsterdam, Netherlands). No customer data is transferred outside the European Economic Area without appropriate safeguards in place.

Legal coverage

The privacy policy and terms & conditions name the software service, company entity (Zody AI Ltd, 17132898) and contact routes consistently across the site.
